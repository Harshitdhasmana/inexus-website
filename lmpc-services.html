About LMPC Certification

What is LMPC?

Legal Metrology for Packaged Commodities (LMPC) registration is required under the Legal Metrology Act, 2009 for any person or entity importing pre-packaged commodities into India. This ensures that all packaged goods meet the prescribed standards for weights, measures, and labeling.

The registration helps maintain consumer protection by ensuring accurate quantity declarations and prevents fraudulent practices in the marketplace.
