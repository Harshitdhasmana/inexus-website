IEC Registration Process

About IEC

Import Export Code (IEC) is a unique 10-digit code issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India.

It's mandatory for any person or entity wanting to engage in import or export of goods and services from/to India.

Key Features:
  • Lifetime validity
  • No renewal required
  • Pan-India coverage
  • Digital certificate available

Required Documents

  • PAN Card (Company/Individual)
  • Bank Certificate/Bank Statement
  • Digital Signature Certificate (DSC)
  • Cancelled Cheque
  • Company Registration Certificate
  • MOA & AOA (for companies)
  • Partnership Deed (for partnerships)
  • Address Proof of Business

Processing Time: 5-7 Working Days

Fast-track processing available for urgent requirements

WPC/ETA Approval Services

WPC Approval

Wireless Planning & Coordination (WPC) approval is mandatory for import, manufacture, sale, or use of wireless equipment operating in radio frequency spectrum in India.

Equipment Coverage:
  • WiFi Routers & Access Points
  • Bluetooth Devices
  • Cellular Mobile Phones
  • Satellite Communication Equipment
  • Radar Systems
  • Radio Frequency Equipment

ETA Approval

Equipment Type Approval (ETA) ensures that telecom equipment meets the technical standards and specifications prescribed by the Telecom Engineering Centre (TEC).

Equipment Types:
  • Telecom Switching Equipment
  • Transmission Equipment
  • Network Equipment
  • Terminal Equipment
  • Optical Fiber Cables
  • Power Equipment
