Why Protect Your Intellectual Property?

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) provide legal protection for your creative and innovative works, giving you exclusive rights to use, license, or sell your intellectual assets.

Proper IPR protection prevents unauthorized use, builds brand value, and creates competitive advantages in the marketplace.

Legal Protection

Exclusive rights and legal protection for your intellectual assets and brand identity

Types of Intellectual Property Rights

Trademark Registration

Protection for brand names, logos, and distinctive signs

  • Word marks and device marks
  • Service marks
  • Collective and certification marks
  • Sound and color trademarks
  • 10-year validity (renewable)
  • Exclusive usage rights

Copyright Registration

Protection for creative and artistic works

  • Literary and artistic works
  • Computer software
  • Musical compositions
  • Films and videos
  • Lifetime + 60 years validity
  • Reproduction and distribution rights

Patent Registration

Protection for inventions and innovations

  • Product patents
  • Process patents
  • Utility models
  • Design patents
  • 20-year validity
  • Exclusive manufacturing rights

Design Registration

Protection for product appearance and aesthetics

  • Industrial designs
  • Product shapes and patterns
  • Ornamental features
  • Visual appeal elements
  • 15-year validity
  • Exclusive design rights

Geographical Indications

Protection for location-specific products

  • Agricultural products
  • Natural goods
  • Manufactured products
  • Traditional crafts
  • 10-year validity (renewable)
  • Regional exclusivity

Plant Variety Protection

Protection for new plant varieties

  • New plant varieties
  • Essentially derived varieties
  • Farmers' varieties
  • Breeder's rights
  • 15-20 year validity
  • Exclusive breeding rights
