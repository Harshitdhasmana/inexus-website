What is FSSAI License?

FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India) license is mandatory for all food business operators in India. It ensures that food products meet safety and quality standards as per Indian regulations.

The license is required for manufacturing, processing, packaging, storing, distributing, or selling food products in India.
