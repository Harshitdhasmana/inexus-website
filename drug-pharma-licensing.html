What is Drug & Pharma Licensing?

Drug and pharmaceutical licensing encompasses all regulatory approvals required for pharmaceutical manufacturing, distribution, clinical trials, and related activities in the pharmaceutical industry.

These licenses ensure compliance with quality standards, safety protocols, and regulatory requirements for pharmaceutical operations in India.

Regulatory Compliance

Essential licenses for pharmaceutical manufacturing and distribution activities

Types of Pharmaceutical Licenses

Drug Manufacturing License

For pharmaceutical manufacturing facilities

  • Form 25 (Small Scale Units)
  • Form 26 (Large Scale Units)
  • Form 29 (Blood Bank License)
  • Schedule M compliance
  • WHO-GMP certification
  • API manufacturing license
Processing: 60-90 Days

Drug Distribution License

For pharmaceutical distribution and wholesale

  • Form 20B (Wholesale License)
  • Form 21B (Retail License)
  • Narcotic & Psychotropic License
  • Vaccine distribution license
  • Cold chain license
  • Online pharmacy license
Processing: 30-45 Days

Clinical Trial License

For clinical research and drug trials

  • Clinical trial permission
  • DCGI approval
  • Ethics committee clearance
  • Subject safety insurance
  • Investigator qualification
  • Protocol review approval
Processing: 90-120 Days

Testing Laboratory License

For pharmaceutical testing facilities

  • Drug testing laboratory
  • NABL accreditation
  • Bioequivalence studies
  • Quality control lab setup
  • Stability testing facility
  • Analytical method validation
Processing: 45-60 Days

Import/Export License

For pharmaceutical import and export

  • Drug import license
  • Free sale certificate
  • Export registration
  • WHO certificate
  • Regulatory dossier
  • Product registration
Processing: 30-60 Days

Specialized Licenses

For specialized pharmaceutical activities

  • Cosmetic manufacturing
  • Medical device license
  • Herbal drug license
  • Ayush drug license
  • Veterinary drug license
  • Radio-pharmaceutical license
Processing: 45-75 Days
