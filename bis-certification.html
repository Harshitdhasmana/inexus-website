BIS Certification

BUREAU OF INDIAN STANDARDS - ISI MARK Registration Services

What is BIS Certification?

BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) certification is a quality assurance mark that ensures products conform to Indian Standards. The ISI Mark is a symbol of safety, reliability, and quality that builds consumer confidence and market acceptance.

BIS certification is mandatory for certain products and voluntary for others, helping manufacturers demonstrate compliance with national standards.

Quality Assurance

Guaranteed product quality and safety standards as per Indian regulations.

Types of BIS Certification

Specialized schemes for diverse product categories.

ISI Mark (Mandatory)

Compulsory certification for specified products like electrical appliances, LPG cylinders, and steel.

ISI Mark (Voluntary)

Optional certification for electronics, textiles, and sports goods to gain competitive advantage.

CRS (Conformity Assessment)

Specific assessment scheme for Electronics, IT goods, Telecom equipment, and Medical devices.

FMCS (Foreign Manufacturer)

Direct certification scheme for overseas manufacturers with global recognition.

The Certification Process

1
Application Submission

Submit application with product details and technical documentation

2
Document Verification

BIS reviews application and verifies all submitted documents

3
Factory Inspection

On-site inspection of manufacturing facility and quality systems

4
Product Testing

Testing of product samples at BIS recognized laboratories

5
Grant of License

Issue of BIS license upon successful completion of all requirements

6
Market Surveillance

Ongoing monitoring and periodic inspections to ensure compliance
