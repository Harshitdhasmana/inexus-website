WPC/ETA APPROVAL

DEPARTMENT OF TELECOMMUNICATIONS EQUIPMENT TYPE APPROVAL

What is WPC/ETA Approval?

WPC (Wireless Planning and Coordination) ETA (Equipment Type Approval) is a mandatory certification from the Department of Telecommunications (DoT), Government of India, for all radio frequency equipment.

This approval ensures that wireless communication equipment meets Indian technical standards and regulatory requirements before being imported, manufactured, or sold in India.

Mandatory Compliance

Required for all wireless equipment operating in Indian radio frequency spectrum.

Equipment Categories

Devices requiring mandatory WPC/ETA certification

Cellular Equipment

  • Mobile phones and smartphones
  • Base transceiver stations
  • Cellular repeaters and boosters
  • Mobile data cards
  • IoT cellular modules
  • M2M communication devices

WiFi & Bluetooth

  • WiFi routers and access points
  • Bluetooth devices
  • WiFi dongles and adapters
  • Wireless keyboards and mice
  • Smart home devices
  • Wireless audio devices

Radio Communication

  • Two-way radios (walkie-talkies)
  • Amateur radio equipment
  • Professional radio systems
  • Satellite communication equipment
  • RF transmitters and transceivers
  • Microwave communication systems

Automotive & Others

  • Car infotainment systems
  • Vehicle tracking devices
  • Remote car starters
  • RFID systems
  • Wireless security systems
  • Medical telemetry devices

WPC/ETA Approval Process

1
Submission

Submit online application with technical specs and test reports.

2
Verification

WPC verifies submitted documents and technical compliance.

3
Testing

Additional testing at TEC approved labs if required by WPC.

4
Evaluation

Detailed technical evaluation by Department of Telecommunications.

5
Fee Payment

Payment of processing fee and other applicable charges.

6
Issuance

Issue of ETA certificate upon successful technical approval.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Technical specifications sheet
  • Block diagram and circuit diagram
  • User manual in English
  • Test reports from TEC approved labs
  • Authorization letter from manufacturer
  • Company registration documents
  • Import/export code certificate
  • Label and marking details
  • Power of attorney (if applicable)

Benefits of WPC/ETA

  • Legal import and marketing in India
  • Compliance with Indian telecom regulations
  • Market access and credibility
  • Customs clearance facilitation
  • Consumer safety assurance
  • Electromagnetic compatibility
  • Interference prevention
  • Brand reputation enhancement
  • Regulatory protection
  • Export eligibility

Testing & Standards

TEC Approved Labs

Testing at TEC approved laboratories including STQC, BIS labs, and ERTL.

Technical Standards

Compliance with Indian ER, ITU-T, IEEE, and IEC specifications.

RF Testing

Performance testing for spurious emissions, RF power, and frequency accuracy.

Safety & EMC

Electrical safety, EMC emissions/immunity, and SAR testing for mobiles.
