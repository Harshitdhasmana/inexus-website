IEC REGISTRATION

MANDATORY 10-DIGIT CODE FOR INTERNATIONAL TRADE OPERATIONS

What is Import Export Code (IEC)?

IEC is a unique 10-digit code mandatory for any person or entity wishing to import or export goods/services from/to India. Issued by the Directorate General of Foreign Trade (DGFT), it serves as the primary identification for international trade transactions.

Without IEC, no import or export is legally permitted except for certain exempted categories under the Foreign Trade Policy.

Lifetime Validity

IEC has lifetime validity with no renewal requirements.

Who Needs IEC Registration?

Business Entities

  • Private Limited Companies
  • Partnership Firms
  • Limited Liability Partnerships (LLP)
  • One Person Companies (OPC)
  • Public Limited Companies
  • Sole Proprietorships
  • Hindu Undivided Families (HUF)
  • Trusts and Societies

Individuals

  • Individual traders and entrepreneurs
  • Freelancers offering export services
  • Consultants providing international services
  • Artists and professionals

Types of Trade

  • Import of goods/raw materials
  • Export of manufactured products
  • Trading in goods
  • Service imports and exports
  • Technology transfer
  • Merchant trading
  • Re-exports
  • Deemed exports
Important Note: Even for a single import/export transaction, IEC is mandatory.
