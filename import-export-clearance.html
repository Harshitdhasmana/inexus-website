Clearance Process Flow

1
Document Verification

Review and verify shipping documents, invoices, and certificates.

2
Classification

Correct HS code determination and duty assessment.

3
Entry Filing

Prepare and file Bill of Entry or Shipping Bill electronically.

4
Payment

Process duty payments and obtain payment receipts.

5
Examination

Coordinate customs examination if required by authorities.

6
Release

Obtain release orders and coordinate final delivery.
