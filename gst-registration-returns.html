GST Registration & Returns

Complete GST compliance services including registration, return filing, and ongoing tax management. Ensure timely compliance with expert support.

What is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is a comprehensive indirect tax system that replaced multiple indirect taxes in India. It's a destination-based tax on consumption of goods and services at a national level.

GST registration is mandatory for businesses with turnover exceeding specified thresholds, and proper compliance ensures smooth business operations.

One Nation, One Tax

Unified tax system simplifying business operations across India

GST Return Filing Types

GSTR-1: Monthly/Quarterly - Outward supplies (Due: 11th of next month)
GSTR-2A: Auto-populated - Inward supplies (Due: 12th of next month)
GSTR-3B: Monthly - Summary return with tax payment (Due: 20th of next month)
GSTR-4: Quarterly - Composition scheme (Due: 18th of next month)
GSTR-5: Monthly - Non-resident taxable person (Due: 20th of next month)
GSTR-6: Monthly - Input service distributor (Due: 13th of next month)
GSTR-7: Monthly - Tax deducted at source (Due: 10th of next month)
GSTR-8: Monthly - Tax collected at source (Due: 10th of next month)
GSTR-9: Annual return - Regular taxpayers (Due: 31st December)
GSTR-9A: Annual return - Composition scheme (Due: 31st December)
GSTR-9C: Annual audit certificate (Due: 31st December)
GSTR-10: Final return - Registration cancellation (Due: Within 3 months)
