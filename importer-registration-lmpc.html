IMPORTER REGISTRATION

LEGAL METROLOGY PACKAGED COMMODITIES (LMPC)

What is LMPC Importer Registration?

LMPC Importer Registration is a mandatory requirement under the Legal Metrology Act, 2009 for any person or entity importing pre-packaged commodities into India. This registration ensures compliance with weight, measure, and labeling standards.

The registration protects consumers by ensuring accurate quantity declarations and prevents fraudulent practices in packaged commodity imports.

Lifetime Validity

Once registered, the LMPC certificate is valid for the lifetime of the business.

Registration Requirements

Who Needs Registration?

  • All importers of pre-packaged commodities
  • Manufacturers importing raw materials for packaging
  • Trading companies dealing with packaged goods
  • E-commerce companies importing packaged products
  • Distributors and wholesalers of imported packaged goods

Covered Products

  • Food and beverage products
  • Pharmaceuticals and cosmetics
  • Consumer goods and electronics
  • Textiles and garments
  • Household and personal care items

Required Documents

1 Import Export Code (IEC) Certificate
2 Company Registration Certificate
3 GST Registration Certificate
4 PAN Card of Company/Proprietor
5 Address Proof of Business Premises
6 List of Products to be Imported
7 Sample Labels/Packaging Details
8 Authorization Letter (if applicable)
