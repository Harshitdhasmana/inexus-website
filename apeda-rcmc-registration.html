What is APEDA/RCMC Registration?

APEDA (Agricultural & Processed Food Products Export Development Authority) registration and RCMC (Registration Cum Membership Certificate) are essential for exporting agricultural and processed food products from India.

These registrations provide access to government schemes, export incentives, and international market opportunities for agricultural exporters.

Export Authorization

Mandatory registration for agricultural and food product exports from India.

APEDA vs RCMC Registration

APEDA Registration

Government export promotion agency focusing on agricultural market development, quality certification, and incentive schemes.

  • Government export promotion agency
  • Agricultural product exports
  • Export incentive schemes
  • Market development support
  • Quality certification
  • International trade facilitation
