NON-STANDARD WEIGHT & MEASUREMENT

SPECIALIZED APPROVAL SERVICES FOR INNOVATIVE PACKAGING DESIGNS

What is Non-Standard Weight Approval?

Non-standard weight and measurement approval is required for products that don't conform to the standard weight categories defined under the Legal Metrology Act. This includes innovative packaging, unique product sizes, or specialized measurement units.

This approval allows manufacturers and importers to market products with non-conventional weights while ensuring consumer protection and regulatory compliance.

Innovation Support

Enables innovative packaging and product designs while maintaining legal compliance.

When is Non-Standard Approval Required?

Non-Standard Weights

  • Products with weights not in standard categories
  • Fractional or decimal weight requirements
  • Products sold by count but requiring weight declaration
  • Bulk packaging with non-standard subdivisions
  • Gift packs and combo packaging

Special Measurements

  • Volume measurements in non-standard units
  • Linear measurements for specific products
  • Area measurements for specialized items
  • Mixed unit declarations (weight + count)
  • Temperature-dependent measurements

Product Categories

  • Pharmaceuticals with dosage-specific packaging
  • Cosmetics and personal care with unique sizes
  • Food products with innovative packaging
  • Electronics with component-based packaging
  • Textiles with length-based measurements
  • Chemicals with concentration-based units
Important Note: Approval must be obtained before manufacturing or importing products with non-standard measurements.

Required Documents

  • Detailed product specifications
  • Technical justification report
  • Product samples or prototypes
  • Manufacturing process details
  • Consumer impact assessment
  • International standards reference
  • Company registration documents
  • Previous approvals (if any)
  • Test certificates from accredited labs
  • Packaging design and labeling

Approval Criteria

  • Technical necessity and innovation
  • Consumer convenience and understanding
  • No deception or fraud potential
  • International practice alignment
  • Market demand and acceptance
  • Measurement accuracy and reliability
  • Clear labeling and declaration
  • Compliance with safety standards
  • Environmental considerations
  • Economic viability and benefit

Types of Non-Standard Approvals

Weight-Based

For products requiring non-standard weight declarations:

  • Fractional weights
  • Combination packaging
  • Multi-unit declarations
Volume-Based

For products with unique volume measurements:

  • Non-standard containers
  • Irregular shapes
  • Temperature-dependent volumes
Dimensional

For products sold by length, area, or dimensions:

  • Textile measurements
  • Sheet materials
  • Cable and wire products
Unit-Based

For products with specialized unit declarations:

  • Count-based packaging
  • Dosage-based products
  • Concentration-based items
