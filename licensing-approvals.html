APEDA/RCMC Registration

About APEDA

Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority (APEDA) is a statutory body responsible for promoting exports of agricultural and processed food products from India.

Products Covered:
  • Fresh Fruits & Vegetables
  • Processed Food Products
  • Meat & Poultry Products
  • Dairy Products
  • Confectionery & Biscuits
  • Alcoholic & Non-alcoholic Beverages
  • Cereal Preparations
  • Miscellaneous Processed Items

RCMC Benefits

Registration-cum-Membership Certificate (RCMC) is mandatory for exporters of products covered under APEDA to avail various benefits and incentives.

Key Benefits:
  • Export Incentives & Subsidies
  • Customs Clearance Support
  • Market Development Assistance
  • Quality Development Support
  • Trade Promotion Programs
  • Export Data & Statistics
  • Grievance Redressal
  • Export Advisory Services

RCMC Processing Time: 15-20 Working Days

Complete registration with ongoing export support and compliance assistance
